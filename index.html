What is Proficient Lawyer?


Proficient Lawyer is a Daily Case Board Management Application for Lawyers and Law Firms. It connects each and every Lawyer in an office along with their Clerks & other Staff Members in a single platform which thereby creates an efficient virual office space for networking.

This application also enables the Lawyers to keep track of the case files at their fingertip and helps the Lawyers in managing their office and case files. The app provides a high end security system for your data stored.

Why Proficient Lawyer?


Proficient Lawyer Info Tech LLP is established by a young entrepreneurial team with a vision to help the legal fraternity to tackle the greatest obstacle that lawyers face in their office life. The goal was to create a platform that could manage an office efficiently, where the lawyers are given an independent space to interact, share and coordinate the daily working of an office seamlessly.

The Proficient Lawyer Info Tech LLP through a thorough Research & Development came up with the application Proficient Lawyer which is soon to be available in all major platforms across the country.
